# Notice

Feng Zikai Illustration (丰子恺水墨插图) is an AI-agent skill created by Mengbooo (https://github.com/Mengbooo).

This skill is inspired by the visual style of Feng Zikai (丰子恺, 1898-1975), one of China's most beloved modern painter-cartoonists. The skill guides AI agents to generate images in Feng Zikai's distinctive ink-wash illustration style — a style deeply rooted in traditional Chinese aesthetics and daily life poetry.

Feng Zikai's original works are in the public domain in many jurisdictions due to their age. This skill does not reproduce or distribute any of Feng Zikai's original works. It encodes stylistic principles to guide AI image generation models.
