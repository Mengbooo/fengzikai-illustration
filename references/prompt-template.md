# 生图提示词模板

**铁律：只有黑墨、灰墨、纸色、一个红印章。没有任何其他颜色。**

---

## 标准模板

```text
BLACK INK childlike drawing on warm paper. Feng Zikai style.

The entire image uses ONLY: black ink, grey ink wash, warm paper (#F0E8D8), one small red seal. NO other colors. No warm washes, no beige, no peach, no blue, no green. This is a monochrome ink drawing, not a colored illustration.

A child drew this with a brush: wobbly, simple, clumsy lines. A figure = circle head + curved body + stick limbs. No face. No details.

Scene: {一句话}

Most of the canvas is empty paper. Very few elements. Simple. Naive.

Hand-brushed calligraphy: {题字}. Uneven, childlike. One red seal.
```
